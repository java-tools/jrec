Note: If the Split Copybook on Redefine field is selected, The system will

  1. Insert a Group record for the copybook.
  2. The copybook will be split the copybook on a redefines clause or 01 level into seperate Child-Records.

Note: Cobol Copybooks are converted to XML copybook by the Open Source project cb2xml (see www.sourceforge.net)

Warning: cb2xml has some bugs at present.
